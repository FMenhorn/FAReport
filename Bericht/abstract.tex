
\begin{abstract}
This paper analyses why Ferienakademie is an ever growing success and gives reasons for interested students why they should participate. By looking at the different methods applied, the authors show the aspects of this summer school which cannot only be reduced to courses but combines them with hikes through beautiful landscapes, sport, cultural and of course social activities. By the end of the conclusion, the reader should be convinced to apply for this great opportunity.
\end{abstract}
