
\begin{abstract}
This paper analyses the monotonically increasing popularity of Ferienakademie and validates the interest it garners from multitudes of students each year . By looking at the key aspects of Ferienakademie, the authors show that the success of this programme results from an optimum combination of courses, excursions into stunning landscapes, sport, and a host of cultural and social activities. By the conclusion of this paper, the authors hope that the reader is convinced of the significance of Ferienakademie, and chooses to take up this great opportunity.
\end{abstract}
